Tutorial Topology Linear Mesh


Goal

The aim of this tutorial is to show the importance of topological component. Indeed, in this tutorial, two linear pendulums are represented, implying:
  • A mechanicalObject with geometry point positions.
  • A mass for gravity and fixed points.
  • A mesh spring forcefield to create springs between points
  • An ODE scheme and a linear solver to solve the system at each time step.

Key points

The only difference between the models is the MeshTopology component. In the first case, edges are defined. Thus, meshSpringForceField will know a list of edges where to create springs. On the other case, now springs are created.
Results

Thus, when simulate, the first model will act as a pendulum, whereas on the other case, points will just fall down as they are not attached together.
