Rigid Mapping

Behavior model Visual model RigidMapping
Description:

A rigid body (perfectly rigid) is an undeformable body. By its proprety undeformable, rigid body composed a set of particles ensuring the distance of any pair of point and the angle of any pair of vectors remains constant in relation to time. Rigid bodies in Sofa are presented by different models (Visual Model, Colision Model, Behaviour Model) ensuring their appropriate task. These model are related by the mapping (rigid mapping in this case).

Sofa Definitions:
  • Model Behaviour Rigid : Position and orientation (quaternion) of the center(s) of mass.
  • Model Colision Rigid : A set of particles used for computing the colision with other objects.
  • Model Visual Rigid : A set of particle used for visualizing.
