Creation of a Pendulum (1/8)


Description:

The objective of this small step by step tutorial is to discover the basic components used with SOFA, how they interact, and how you can configure them. We will create a pendulum made of two objects, and two different springs. We will not go into details on the template used for the mechanical states, or the forcefields used.

Components:

We used 3 components:
  • A Node "FirstObject" to store the components describing our object we want to simulate. It is important that the hierarchy of your nodes in the scene graph reflects the scene you want to model.
  • The shape is modeled using a MechanicalObject which desribes the particle states.
  • A UniformMass is added for each particle, giving for each particle of the MechanicalObject a mass.
