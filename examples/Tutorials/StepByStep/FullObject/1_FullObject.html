Creation of a Scene (2/9)


Description

We add a mechanical object with a mass and a solver to simulate our deformable body. See this tutorial
Key points

An internal force field is added, the PlaneForceField. It simulates a plane (direction + distance to the origin). As a start, we will simulate the collision with a floor using this kind of "trick". Later, we will see how to put any object as an obstacle.
Results

A particule falls along the direction of the gravity, and remains on the plane defined by the PlaneForceField.
Related

Modify the parameters of the PlaneForceField, and understand your changes.
